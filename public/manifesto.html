the bio/acc manifesto

1. decentralize biopower

Biotechnology must not remain locked within institutions. We distribute research, funding, and infrastructure across open networks. No gatekeepers. No permission.

2. engineer ethical evolution

Acceleration without alignment leads to collapse. We embed biosafety, transparency, and accountability into every system we build.

3. align with ecological intelligence

Nature is not a constraint — it is the blueprint. We design systems that cooperate with biological reality rather than override it.

4. hack health

Biology is programmable. Individuals should have access to tools that allow them to understand and improve their bodies and minds.

5. open-source the code of life

Genomes, therapies, and protocols belong in the commons. Closed systems slow progress. Open systems accelerate it.

6. accelerate biotech infrastructure

From lab automation to decentralized manufacturing, we reduce the time between discovery and deployment.

7. build in public

Transparency creates trust. We document, share, and iterate openly so others can build with us.

8. evolve or expire

The future will not wait. We either accelerate biotechnology responsibly — or fall behind those who will.
